Who We Are

India Company, 3rd Battalion, 5th Marines is a realism MILSIM unit focused on historical accuracy and disciplined, immersive gameplay in Arma 3.

Our Mission

We portray the United States Marine Corps and their combat operations during historical conflicts such as World War II, the Vietnam War, and Korea.

With several unit members currently serving in the United States Marine Corps or having served in the Marine Corps in the past, as well as thousands of hours of historical research, veteran interviews and virtual experience dedicated to reenacting Marines in historical conflicts, we aim to provide the most accurate, realistic, and professional portrayal of the Infantry Marine during these conflicts.

Requirements to Join

ACTIVITY: Make ops on Thursday and Saturday, be on time, and let us know in advance if you can’t make it! We also have plenty of other games and side operations we play during the week to help build community.

GOOD ATTITUDE: Be positive, be friendly, be professional. It’s a game, let’s not bring drama into it!

PASSION FOR HISTORY: A lot of us are passionate about history, and portraying history as accurately as possible! Bring a love for history, Arma MilSim, or a desire to be a part of a tight-knit community along with you!

Ready to Join?
